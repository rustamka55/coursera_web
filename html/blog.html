(G)I-DLE released their new EP

by S.Somebody

12.01.2021

On Monday (01/11), the K-Pop 6-piece girl group dropped their fourth mini album I burn via CUBE Entertainment and Republic Records.
I burn includes self-written and self-produced tracks by (G)I-dle with songwriting and arranegement collaborations with Pop Time, BreadBeat, Seo Jae-woo, FCM Houdini, FCM 667, Lee Woo-min (a.k.a. collapsedone), and Ahn Ye-eun.
Three concept versions of I burn, namely Winter, Flower, and Fire, are available and each come with a sleeve, booklet, CD, lyric paper, mini poster, postcard, photo card, lucky card, and sticker.
The 6-track offering features the lead single '화(火花)(HWAA)', which also received its own visual alongside the mini album's release.
